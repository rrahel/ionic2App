By default, physical interface (used for VXLAN tunneling) has an MTU of 1500 octets and when LB agent creates a VXLAN interface, the MTU is automatically 50 octets less than the physical interface (so 1450). Therefore, the VXLAN interface has an MTU of 1450 octets. So the bridge could only forward packets of length smaller than 1450 octets to VXLAN interface. But the interfaces used to link network namespaces and bridges are spawn by dhcp agents (and other agents) with an MTU of 1500 octets. {{selectedItem.description}}
